## Part 2. Describe a Picture

### Q3 — 거의 다 맞았습니다

**내 원문:**
> This picture was taken at an airport. i can see 2 people, who seem to be walking and chatting, i think they are looking at the background, in the background there are 2 airplanes, they are parked in a row. overal it looks like a typical airport scene.

이 답변은 be동사, 단복수, 문장 구조까지 전반적으로 깔끔했습니다. 딱 한 군데만 손보면 됩니다.

- `i think they are looking at the background` → `i think they are looking outside toward the airplanes`
- **무엇이 바뀌었는지:** `background`는 "사진 안에서 우리가 보는 배경"을 가리키는 말이라, 사진 속 인물이 "배경을 본다"고 하면 어색합니다 (그 사람들 입장에선 배경이 아니라 그냥 눈앞의 풍경이니까요). "그들이 (창밖/비행기 쪽을) 바라보는 것 같다"처럼 인물 시점에서 자연스럽게 표현하면 됩니다.

### Q4

**내 원문:**
> This pickture was taken at a restaurant. i can see 4 people. 3 of them seem to be clinets. they are looking at a food. on the right side, i can see 1 poeple who seems to be a server. he seesm to be giving a food to them. on the table, there are some items in the background. i can see some items displayed on the sheles overal it looks like a typical restaurant.

**교정 문장:**
> This picture was taken at a restaurant. I can see four people. Three of them seem to be customers, and they are looking at the food. On the right side, I can see one person who seems to be a server — he seems to be serving them food. There are some items on the table, and in the background, I can see some items displayed on the shelves. Overall, it looks like a typical restaurant scene.

**무엇이 바뀌었는지:**
- `clinets` 자리에 쓴 단어 자체 — 식당 손님은 `clients`(고객, 주로 변호사·컨설턴트 등 전문 서비스의 고객)가 아니라 `customers`(손님)입니다.
- `looking at a food` / `giving a food` → `the food` / `food`: `food`는 셀 수 없는 명사라 `a`를 붙이지 않습니다.
- `1 poeple` → `one person`: `people`는 이미 복수형이라 "한 명"은 `one person`이라고 해야 합니다.
- `giving a food to them` → `serving them food`: 웨이터의 동작은 `give`보다 `serve`가 훨씬 자연스러운 콜로케이션입니다.
- `on the table, there are some items in the background` → 원문이 "테이블 위에"와 "배경에"를 한 문장에 섞어서 헷갈렸는데, 두 위치를 분리해서 정리했습니다.

## Part 3. Respond to Questions

### Q5

**내 원문:**
> i travel for leisure for twice a year, and i usually go to jeju island. there a lots of to do lesure things.

**교정 문장:**
> I travel for leisure twice a year, and I usually go to Jeju Island. There are a lot of leisure activities to do there.

**무엇이 바뀌었는지:**
- `for twice a year` → `twice a year`: `twice`, `three times` 같은 빈도 표현 앞에는 `for`를 붙이지 않습니다.
- `there a lots of to do lesure things` → `there are a lot of leisure activities to do`: `are` 누락, `a lots of`가 아니라 `a lot of`(lot은 단수), 그리고 어순도 "~할 것들"은 `things to do` 순서로.

### Q6

**내 원문:**
> in my case, price is the most important factor when choosing a vacation destination. because i'm student so i want to go something reasonable place.

**교정 문장:**
> In my case, price is the most important factor when choosing a vacation destination, because I'm a student, so I want to go somewhere reasonably priced.

**무엇이 바뀌었는지:**
- `i'm student` → `I'm a student`: 관사 `a` 누락.
- `go something reasonable place` → `go somewhere reasonably priced`: "적당한 가격의 장소"는 `somewhere reasonably priced` 또는 `a reasonably priced place`로 표현합니다.

### Q7 — 구조는 이제 완벽합니다, 문장만 조금 다듬었습니다

**내 원문:**
> first, i think people book lfight and hotels online themselves is better than using travel agencies. because the travel agencies get some extra fees to serve their services to reserve the flight and accomodations. and seceont for my advice, i think travel agencies have to get some feedback from the travelers who experience their services and make it better than before.

**교정 문장:**
> First, I think booking flights and hotels online yourself is better than using travel agencies, because travel agencies charge extra fees for their booking services. And second, for my advice, I think travel agencies should collect feedback from travelers who have used their services and improve based on that.

**무엇이 바뀌었는지:**
- `people book flight and hotels online themselves is better than` → `booking flights and hotels online yourself is better than`: 주어 자리에는 동사원형(book)이 아니라 동명사(booking)가 와야 문법적으로 맞습니다.
- `get some extra fees to serve their services to reserve` → `charge extra fees for their booking services`: 원래 문장이 "서비스를 제공하기 위해 수수료를 받는다"는 식으로 꼬여 있어서, "예약 서비스에 대해 수수료를 부과한다"는 뜻을 더 직접적으로 표현했습니다.
- `who experience their services` → `who have used their services`: 이미 겪은 경험이므로 현재완료 시제가 맞습니다.
- **바꾸지 않은 부분:** `and second for my advice`라는 연결 표현은 문법적으로 전혀 문제없어서 그대로 뒀습니다. (참고로 `As for advice, ...`도 같은 역할을 하는 표현이라, 둘 다 쓰셔도 됩니다.)

## Part 4. Respond to Questions Using Information Provided

### Q8

**내 원문:**
> it takes 65dollars per person, and it can hold 40.

**교정 문장:**
> It costs $65 per person, and it can hold 40 people.

**무엇이 바뀌었는지:**
- `it takes 65 dollars` → `it costs $65`: 가격을 말할 땐 `take`가 아니라 `cost`를 씁니다 (`take`는 시간/노력에 씀).

---

### Q9

**내 원문:**
> yes that's right, they have some activities about cooking class and live music, it takes 30minutes to go.

**교정 문장:**
> Yes, that's right. They have some activities, such as a cooking class and live music, and it takes 30 minutes to get there.

**무엇이 바뀌었는지:**
- `activities about cooking class and live music` → `activities, such as a cooking class and live music`: 활동을 나열할 땐 `about`이 아니라 `such as`/`including`을 씁니다.

### Q10 — 정답이었습니다, 표현만 하나 다듬었습니다

**내 원문:**
> in this case, i would recoment riverside hall, it can hold 60 people and it takes only 75 dollars, and it has cooking class and live music activities. also it takes only 30 minutes to go.

**교정 문장:**
> In this case, I would recommend Riverside Hall. It can hold 60 people and costs only $75, and it has cooking class and live music activities. Also, it only takes 30 minutes to get there.

**무엇이 바뀌었는지:**
- `it takes only 75 dollars` → `it costs only $75`: Q8과 같은 `take`/`cost` 패턴이 여기서도 반복됐습니다 — 이번 세트에서 새로 눈에 띄는 반복 실수입니다.
- 조건(인원 50명 이상, 가격 $80 미만)을 둘 다 확인해서 Riverside Hall을 정확히 골라낸 건 완벽했습니다.

## Part 5. Express an Opinion

### Q11

**내 원문:**
> I think investing more in publick transportation is better than funding on roads and infrascturcture for cars. there are some reasons that support my opionion,. first recently economy has dropped ,as few people can buy cars. second it takes more time to fix road infrastructure than transportation infrascturcture. last it pollutes if more cars on the road. so i think this is why i prefer investing more in public transportation.

**교정 문장:**
> I think investing more in public transportation is better than funding roads and infrastructure for cars. There are some reasons that support my opinion. First, recently the economy has dropped, so fewer people can afford cars. Second, it takes more time to fix road infrastructure than public transportation infrastructure. Last, it pollutes more if there are more cars on the road. So, this is why I prefer investing more in public transportation.

**무엇이 바뀌었는지:**
- `funding on roads` → `funding roads`: `fund`는 타동사라 뒤에 바로 목적어가 오고, `on`이 필요 없습니다.
- `economy has dropped` → `the economy has dropped`: 특정 대상(경제 상황)을 가리킬 땐 `the` 필요.
- `it pollutes if more cars on the road` → `it pollutes more if there are more cars on the road`: `there are` 누락 (존재문에서 be동사가 빠지는 패턴이 이번에도 한 번 나왔습니다).
- 세 가지 이유(경제/유지보수 시간/환경오염)로 논지를 전개한 구조는 좋았습니다 — First/Second/Last로 순서를 명확히 표시한 것도 도움이 됩니다.
