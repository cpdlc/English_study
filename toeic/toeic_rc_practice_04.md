### Single Passage 3 — Article

**Riverside Manufacturing Wins Regional Safety Excellence Award**

Riverside Manufacturing was honored last week with the Regional Safety Excellence Award, recognizing the company's plant for operating more than 1,000 consecutive days without a lost-time workplace injury. The award, presented annually by the Regional Manufacturers Association, is given to only one facility each year among more than 200 eligible plants in the region.

Plant manager Felix Ortega credited the achievement to a company-wide safety culture built over the past three years, including daily safety briefings, an anonymous hazard-reporting system, and quarterly training refreshers for all floor staff. "This award reflects the effort of every single person on our floor, not just the safety team," Ortega said in a statement following the ceremony.

The Regional Manufacturers Association noted that Riverside's hazard-reporting system, which allows employees to flag potential safety issues without providing their names, was cited as a key differentiator among nominated facilities. Since its introduction, reported near-misses have increased by nearly 60 percent, a trend the association considers a sign of stronger safety awareness rather than declining safety performance.

Riverside Manufacturing plans to share details of its safety program with other facilities in its parent company's network in the coming months.
